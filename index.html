Welcome!

Kevin is an undergraduate at Cornell University (graduating in 2024), studying computer science, with interests in computer vision and human-machine interfaces. He is currently the computer vision lead for the Cornell University AutoBoat project team, and he has previously worked as a research intern for the METEOR Studio at Arizona State University and an IT consultant for the Institute for Compensation Studies at the Cornell ILR School. He also has experience as a teaching assistant for several courses at Cornell University, including CS 3410 (Computer Systems Organization), CS 2800 (Discrete Structures), and CS 2112 (Honors Object Oriented Programming). Ultimately, Kevin is interested in leveraging his technical expertise and creative spirit towards networking, learning, and career opportunities in the summer of 2024.
