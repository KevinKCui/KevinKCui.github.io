Welcome!

My name is Kevin Cui, and I am an undergraduate/masters of engineering student at Cornell University (graduating in 2024), studying computer science. I am currently the computer vision lead for the Cornell University AutoBoat project team, and I have previously worked as a research intern for the METEOR Studio at Arizona State University and an IT consultant for the Institute for Compensation Studies at the Cornell ILR School. I also have experience as a teaching assistant for several courses at Cornell University, including CS 3410 (Computer Systems Organization), CS 2800 (Discrete Structures), and CS 2112 (Honors Object Oriented Programming). Ultimately, I am interested in leveraging my technical expertise and creative spirit towards networking, learning, and career opportunities, especially in the fields of computer vision and human-machine interfaces.
